Intent of the Act

Pre-Conception And Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques (Prohibition Of Sex Selection) Act, 1994

Intent of the Act

The Pre-Conception and Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques (Prohibition of Sex Selection) Act, 1994 was enacted with the following primary objectives:

●

To prohibit sex selection and to regulate the use and to prevent misuse of pre-natal diagnostic techniques

●

To prohibit the selection of sex using procedures

●

Relatives and the husband of a pregnant woman are prohibited from seeking or encouraging the conduct of sex-selection techniques. Section 2(o), 3A and 4

Legal Framework

This Act provides a comprehensive legal framework to prevent gender-based discrimination and protect the rights of the girl child by regulating medical diagnostic procedures.
