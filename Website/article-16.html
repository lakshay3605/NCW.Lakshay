Article 16 - Equal Opportunity in Employment

Constitutional guarantee of equal opportunity in public employment

Constitutional Text

"There shall be equality of opportunity for all citizens in matters relating to employment or appointment to any office under the State."

Key Provisions

  • Equal Opportunity: All citizens have equal right to public employment
  • Merit-based Selection: Appointments based on qualifications and merit
  • Non-discrimination: No discrimination in government jobs based on sex
  • Reservation Provisions: Allows for affirmative action in employment

Women's Employment Rights

Article 16 ensures that women have equal access to government positions and cannot be discriminated against in public employment based on their gender.

Special Provisions for Women

  • Equal consideration for all government positions
  • Protection against gender-based discrimination in recruitment
  • Foundation for women's reservation in government jobs
  • Equal promotion opportunities in public service

Implementation

This article has been instrumental in ensuring women's participation in civil services, police forces, judiciary, and other government positions, breaking traditional gender barriers in public employment.

Current Status

Today, women serve in all levels of government, from entry-level positions to the highest offices, including as judges, IAS officers, police commissioners, and cabinet ministers, thanks to the protection provided by Article 16.
