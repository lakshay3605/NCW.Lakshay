Article 16 - Equal Opportunity in Employment

Constitutional guarantee of equal opportunity in public employment

Constitutional Text

"There shall be equality of opportunity for all citizens in matters relating to employment or appointment to any office under the State."

Key Provisions

•

Equal Opportunity: All citizens have equal right to seek employment in government services.

•

Merit-Based Selection: Appointments should be based on qualifications and merit, not gender or other discriminatory factors.

•

Non-Discrimination: No citizen can be denied employment solely on grounds of religion, race, caste, sex, or place of birth.

Benefits for Women

•

Government Jobs: Equal access to all government positions and civil services for women.

•

Career Advancement: Equal opportunities for promotion and career growth in public sector.

•

Leadership Roles: Access to senior administrative and judicial positions without gender bias.

Reservations and Special Provisions

Article 16(4) allows the State to make provisions for reservation in appointments or posts in favor of any backward class of citizens, including women in certain sectors, to ensure adequate representation.

Current Implementation

•

Women in Civil Services: Increasing participation of women in IAS, IPS, and other central services.

•

Defense Services: Recent inclusion of women in combat roles and permanent commission.

•

Judicial Services: Equal opportunity for women in lower and higher judiciary positions.
