Is advertisement of pre-conception and pre-natal determination of sex permissible

Complete Ban on Advertisements

The Act strictly forbids and imposes penalties for any form of advertisement related to pre-conception and pre-natal sex determination. This includes a complete ban on the issuance, publication, or communication of such advertisements through any medium.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Any advertisement promoting sex determination services is completely prohibited under this Act, regardless of the medium used for communication.
