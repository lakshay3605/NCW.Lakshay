Panchayati Raj Institutions & Urban Local Bodies

Constitutional provisions for women's participation in local governance

Women's Reservation in Panchayati Raj

Constitutional amendments ensuring 33% reservation for women in Panchayati Raj Institutions, empowering them to participate in grassroots democracy and local governance.

  • Reservation in Gram Panchayats
  • Reservation in Panchayat Samitis
  • Reservation in Zilla Panchayats
  • Leadership roles and chairperson positions
Learn More

Urban Local Bodies

Similar provisions for women's participation in urban governance through Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils, and Nagar Panchayats.

  • Municipal Corporation representation
  • Municipal Council participation
  • Nagar Panchayat involvement
  • Mayor and chairperson positions
