Qualification of Medical Practitioner

Registered Medical Practitioner (RMP) Definition

A Registered Medical Practitioner (RMP) for the purposes of this Act is defined as a medical professional who fulfills all of the following criteria:

●

Possesses a medical qualification recognized under the Indian Medical Council Act, 1956.

●

Is duly registered, with their name entered in a State Medical Register.

●

Has the prescribed experience or training in the field of gynaecology and obstetrics as specified by the rules of this Act.
